Story

You are a student wizard undergoing trials to prove yourself a master wizard. In this trial, you must venture to the Castle to Hell to defeat the Demon Overlord. The path to the Castle to Hell is long and rigorous. You must walk through the Open Fields to get to the Archway Cave. Past the cave will be the Weeping Bones Necrofield graveyard. The Castle to Hell lies just beyond. Every location will have its own mix of enemies, so use your 3 health and mana potions wisely. Get to the Castle of Hell and defeat the Demon Overlord to prove yourself!

Intructions

You will fight through 4 zones. Each has 4 random encounters followed by a boss battle. To begin the next battle, press "Start" on the bottom right once the game begins. You can choose to attack, use magic or an item on your turn. You will gain experience after each battle and level up after a certain amount is gained. Spells will become stronger as you use them more often and deal 1.5 times as much damage if an enemy is weak to the element. Should you happen to be defeated in battle at any point, you will restart the journey from the beginning but reatain your current level and spell progress.

Have fun!
